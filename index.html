Do you support multiple domains per AdWords account?

Yes! Spend will be summed and calculated for all websites together.

What are your available payment methods?

We support all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, AMEX, Maestro and Discover), Bitcoin and PayPal.

What if I cut my AdWords Budget to be lower?

It’s very easy to downgrade your package in the user dashboard.

Do I have to sign a 12 month contract?

We don’t make you sign any yearly or quarterly contracts and all our packages are month to month, which means you can cancel at any time.

Do you support MCC accounts?

Yes. All of our packages are limited through spend. This means your linked adWords account can have as many campaigns and website as you need within your spend.

What if my AdWords budget goes over my limit?

If your adWords budget exceeds your plan’s maximum then we’ll only protect up to the maximum for your current plan. We’ll then send you an email notifying you and suggesting you upgrade your subscription. The payment for your updated subscription will be calculated depending on the days left in your monthly billing period.
